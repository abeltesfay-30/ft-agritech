About FT Agritech Solutions

Born from Lived Experience

FT Agritech was founded by Yordanos Abay, inspired by the reality faced by countless Ethiopian farming families — children spending long days guarding farms while 30–70% of harvest is lost to animal intrusion and pests.

Farmers lose over 240 kg of maize per household (~34%), with some regions reporting losses exceeding 75%. This isn't just about economics — it's about food security, education, and the future of rural communities.

Our Mission

Modernizing Ethiopian agriculture through locally-developed technology, aligned with Digital Ethiopia 2030 and empowering farmers with smart, affordable solutions.

The Challenge Ethiopian Farmers Face

🐾

Animal Intrusion

Wild animals and livestock destroy crops, especially during night hours when farms are unguarded.

🐛

Pest Infestation

Pests attack crops without early warning, causing devastating losses before farmers can respond.

⚠️

No Early Detection

Traditional methods lack real-time monitoring, leaving farms vulnerable to threats.

👨‍🌾

Manual Guarding

Children and family members spend exhausting hours guarding fields instead of attending school.

The Result:

  • Reduced income and economic instability
  • Heavy labor burden on families
  • Food insecurity and malnutrition
  • Lost educational opportunities for children
