The Challenge Ethiopian Farmers Face

🐾

Animal Intrusion

Wild animals and livestock destroy crops, especially during night hours when farms are unguarded.

🐛

Pest Infestation

Pests attack crops without early warning, causing devastating losses before farmers can respond.

⚠️

No Early Detection

Traditional methods lack real-time monitoring, leaving farms vulnerable to threats.

👨‍🌾

Manual Guarding

Children and family members spend exhausting hours guarding fields instead of attending school.

The Result:

  • Reduced income and economic instability
  • Heavy labor burden on families
  • Food insecurity and malnutrition
  • Lost educational opportunities for children
